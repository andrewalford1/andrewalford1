Lego affilated volunteer

Brick Alley Lego user group

Brick Alley is one of the few RLUGs (Recognised Lego User Groups) in the UK, as part of the group I build and display large-scale Lego models which are showcased in meseums and fan conventions all over the country. This is all in an effort to raise money for our selected children's charities: Fairy Bricks & Holy Rood House.
